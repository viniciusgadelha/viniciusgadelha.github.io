About me

I work at the intersection of power systems, data science, and grid modeling. My focus is on designing practical, data-driven methods to support distribution network operation and planning — from KPI frameworks and impact assessment to digital twins and flexibility analysis.

Data-driven evaluation

Designing KPIs and evaluation frameworks to assess technical, economic, and operational performance across multiple sites.

Digital twins & GIS

Building digital twins of distribution grids from GIS topology data, ensuring traceability and alignment with field configurations.

ML + power flow

Combining statistical methods, machine learning, and system modeling to extract insights from real-world datasets and support grid decisions.
