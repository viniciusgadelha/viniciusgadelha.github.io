About me

I’m a Postdoctoral Researcher at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC) with a strong background in advanced analytics, machine learning, and data-driven decision support for complex, real-world systems. Over the last years I have worked on building evaluation frameworks, defining and monitoring KPIs, and translating heterogeneous data into clear insights and actionable reporting.

My work sits at the intersection of data science and engineering: integrating diverse data sources (e.g., GIS topology and high-frequency measurements), developing analytical workflows, and producing structured deliverables for multi-partner projects. I enjoy roles where I can combine rigorous analysis with practical impact—turning data into decisions that improve outcomes.

I have an international academic and professional path across Spain, Italy, Germany, and Brazil. I’m comfortable collaborating in cross-disciplinary environments and communicating technical results to both technical and non-technical audiences.
