About me

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Electrical Engineering at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), under the supervision of Mónica Aragüés Peñalba and Sara Barja-Martinez. My research focuses on integrating artificial intelligence into power systems to enhance flexibility, real-time grid operations, and reduce carbon emissions. Specifically, I focus on enhancing load flexibility and demand response to optimize energy systems, improve the economic efficiency of renewable energy integration, and support the integration of electric vehicles into the grid.

My work combines AI-driven optimization models with the physical dynamics of power grids to ensure both computational efficiency and practical reliability. Recently, I developed a Federated Learning-based short-term load forecasting model, enabling privacy-preserving collaboration and tailored predictions through clustering. These efforts contribute to the deployment of cost-effective and sustainable energy technologies.
